I. 1. Introduction to Screenwriting

I'm sure you all know and maybe have at least one favourite movie or more. They have all kind of sorts of scenes, no? Well, let me tell you that all of the shots used there aren't coincidental, and it's already written on a piece of writing—all specific character or camera movements, or even the settings of the story.

Screenwriting is the process of writing scripts for movies, television shows, video games, and even web series. A screenwriter crafts the story, the characters, and the dialogue that will eventually come to life on the screen. A screenplay acts as a guide for the entire filmmaking process. It provides descriptions of scenes, outlines character actions, and details every line of dialogue the actors will speak.
